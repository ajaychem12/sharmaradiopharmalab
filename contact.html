Lab Inquiries

We welcome inquiries from prospective students, postdocs, and collaborators interested in radiopharmaceutical development, molecular imaging, and cancer theranostics. Please feel free to reach out via email to discuss potential opportunities.

Research Areas of Interest:

  • PET imaging agent development
  • Radiotherapeutic design and synthesis
  • Peptide-based targeting strategies
  • Ovarian and pancreatic cancer research
  • Multiple myeloma and neuroendocrine tumor imaging
  • Translational molecular imaging
  • Theranostic development using various radionuclides (18F, 64Cu, 68Ga, 177Lu, 225Ac)
