Welcome to the Sharma Lab

We are a multidisciplinary research team at Johns Hopkins University focused on developing novel PET imaging agents and targeted radiopharmaceuticals for the diagnosis and treatment of cancer. Led by Dr. Ajay Kumar Sharma, our work bridges chemistry, molecular imaging, and oncology to advance precision medicine with a strong emphasis on ovarian and pancreatic cancer.

Our lab integrates radiochemistry, imaging, and translational oncology to develop precision diagnostic and therapeutic tools.
